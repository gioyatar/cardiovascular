WHAT ARE ORGAN SYSTEM AND WHY THE CARDIOVASCULAR SYSTEM IS IMPORTANT?

Organ System are an organized groups of organs that work together to perform a specific task in our body. Every organ has a special role just like the cardiovascular system. The cardiovascular system plays an important role in our body being its ‘transport network’ which transports the oxygen, hormones, and nutrients throughout our body and removes waste like carbon dioxide.




PURPOSE OF THIS SITE

This website was created with the purpose of providing a clear and informative overview of the cardiovascular system and its vital role in the human body. It aims to help students and readers understand the structure, function, and importance of this essential system.
